OUR MISSION AND AIM

The mission or thought behind our work is that every creature on earth deserve a healthy , natural and happy life weather it is humans or animals all deserve best. Our main aim is to provide help , first aid , food , shelter (if required), to all those stray animals who are rejected , in need , injured, not getting proper environment and food. Also to spread awareness among people so that they can contribute as much as can to show their humanity in a noble cause.
